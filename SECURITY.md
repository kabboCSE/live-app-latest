# 🛡️ Security Policy & DMCA Takedown Guidelines

Welcome to the IPTV Player repository. This document outlines our security reporting guidelines, disclaimer, and policies regarding copyright and DMCA inquiries.

We are committed to resolving security concerns and copyright issues quickly, responsibly, and amicably. Please read below to understand why this policy exists and how to contact us.

---

## 📋 Why This Policy Exists

This policy is established to:
1. **Provide a direct communication channel** for copyright owners to request stream removals, preventing unnecessary legal complexity.
2. **Promote responsible disclosure** of security vulnerabilities to protect our users and system integrity.
3. **Clarify compliance and limitations** of this IPTV client software.

---

## 🚫 1. DMCA & Copyright Infringement Policy

### Disclaimer
This repository (`SHAJON-404/iptv`) is a client-side player. **We do not host, store, stream, or retransmit any media files, video streams, or channels on our servers.** 
The database files (`.json` and `.m3u` playlists) contain only reference links that are publicly accessible on the internet.

### "Contact First" Policy
If you are a copyright owner or an agent representing a copyright owner and believe that any content linked within our playlists infringes upon your copyrights, **we kindly request that you contact us directly first**. 

By contacting us directly, we can bypass lengthy administrative processes and **remove the infringing links immediately (typically within 24–48 hours)**. Filing a formal DMCA notice with GitHub or hosting providers takes longer and creates unnecessary overhead for both parties, whereas a direct message resolves the issue instantly.

### How to Request a Link Removal
To request the removal of a link, please send a message with the following details:
1. **Infringing Link**: The exact channel name or streaming link from our JSON/M3U playlist that you wish to be removed.
2. **Proof of Ownership**: A brief statement or evidence showing that you are the rightful owner or authorized representative of the copyrighted material.
3. **Contact Info**: Your name and contact details (email or profile).

You can reach us through the official contact channels listed below.

## 🔒 2. Security Vulnerability Reporting

If you discover a security vulnerability in this application (such as remote code execution, XSS, caching vulnerabilities, or sensitive data leakage), please **do not open a public issue** on GitHub. Publicly disclosing vulnerabilities exposes all users to potential exploits before a patch can be developed.

### How to Report a Vulnerabilities
Please report any security concerns privately via our direct contact channels. 

When reporting, please include:
- A description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue (proof-of-concept code or screenshots if applicable).
- Potential impact of the exploit.

We will acknowledge your report, investigate the issue, and release a fix as soon as possible.
